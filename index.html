Hi, I’m Jia Jun

Welcome to my website!

I'm Jia Jun, a Year 2 undergraduate studying at NUS. I'm majoring in Statistics as I'm interested in probability and statistics. Initially, I was interested in physics but after a questionable experience in PC1101, it seems like I shall not pursue it any further. I hope you'll enjoy looking at this website!

Interests

Statistics

I'm interested in Applied Probability and Statistics, with a focus in stochastic processes and bayesian statistics. My long term goal is to study measure theory to gain a deeper understanding of probability theory, though I'll need to build up my knowledge on analysis first. I'll slowly work my way to this goal in the later years of my undergraduate studies.

Music

Admittedly, I can't play any musical instruments (unless you count the recorder lessons during primary school) but that doesn't stop me from listening to music in my free time. My favourite band is Linkin Park as their songs resonate with me. Their music captures a wide variety of emotions and explores many themes, allowing everyone to interpret them in a personal way. My favourite song usually depends on how I feel at the moment but if I had to name one, it would be Forgotten from their Hybrid Theory album.

IEMs

I was part of the Audio Visual Aid club for 6 years, hence I've developed an interest in audio technology. As a result, I spent quite some time finding the correct equipment to enhance my listening experience. My favourite IEM that I own is the Moondrop Blessing 3. I will not elaborate on how much money I've spent on this hobby.

Mechanical Pencils

There’s something oddly charming about how the Japanese overengineer their stationery. I find it very cool how a simple piece of writing equipment can have so many interesting features. Some examples are the Kuru Toga lead rotation, Delguard lead protection system and the Orenz Nero automatic lead extension system. I got a little too into it. Now I carry 6 mechanical pencils around, and I own 17 in total.
