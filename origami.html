Works That I Have Folded

I mainly use A4 coloured paper for these origami or some single sided origami paper from Daiso because I'm poor...(I'm actually just lazy to go out and buy origami paper so I just use what's lying around at home). I personally feel that the stuff that I have folded are all beginner or intermediate level, so most people should be able to achieve the same results as me with some practice. I've actually folded more works, but unfortunately I did not take a photo and lost them ;-;

Most of the tutorials I follow are by Jo Nakashima. His tutorials are extremely beginner friendly and also include a diagram on the top right showing what folds are being made. I would highly reccomend people just starting out to follow his tutorials!
